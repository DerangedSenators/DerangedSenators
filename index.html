Cops and Robbers





Set in America 2020, a country full of opportunities, people, and wealth. Having an abundance of wealth means more than ever. Nice cars, nice mansion and most importantly FAME! It is time for The Robbers to come out of their hideouts and acquire some fame for themselves. On the other hand, The Cops’ numbers and their ways of suppressing criminals are now more tremendous than ever.


In this cardinal battle between The Cops and The Robbers, whose side will you pick?
